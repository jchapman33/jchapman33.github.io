Being a ScrumMaster

Sep 23, 2016

CONTEXT:
While giving some thought as to how to give back to the community of Agile practitioners, there was a consensus there was a need for helping ScrumMasters understand that they’re on the right track. The following videos aim to help ScrumMasters as they’re working with teams.

DESCRIPTION OF VIDEOS:

  • Video length: 15 to 20-minutes
  • Season #1: 5 Videos

TOPICS DISCUSSED:

  • Advice on planning: e.g. Velocity, Cycle-time, no-estimate
  • Facilitating a quality: Retrospective, stand-up, etc.
  • Evaluation of ScrumMasters e.g. introspection)
  • Tools and tips for being a Servent Leader

SEASON ONE:


EPISODE: 101 - An in-person conversation with Stephen Vance. Our original conversation was greater then 1hour 20minutes, this is the edited version. If you’re interested in the full conversation please email me with your request.

EPISODE: 102 - A Skype conversation with Michael de la Maza, because Michael and I are on opposite coasts. This was is a conversation that ran about 45-minutes and is edited to 19-minutes. If you’re interested in the full conversation please email me with your request.

EPISODE: 103 - An in-person conversation with David Grabel reviewing the Agile Mindset and what it means for ScrumMasters. Roughly a 16-minute interview.

EPISODE: 104 - A Zoom conversation with Tarang Baxi, a conversation that discusses how to use Lean and design thinking concepts.

EPISODE: 105 - A Zoom conversation with Richard Kasperowski, shares his list of 5 things for helping to prepare yourself to being a good team coach/ScrumMaster.
